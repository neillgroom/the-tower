How Verification Works

Verification is independent and mathematical. No trust required.

1. Your text is hashed with SHA-256 — a one-way function.

2. The hash is checked against The Tower's database.

3. If matched, the timestamp is verified against the Bitcoin blockchain.

4. Anyone can independently verify — no access to The Tower needed.
